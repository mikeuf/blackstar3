Instructions

This is the easiest game ever (for now). Just control your manatee with the arrow keys and eat all of the cabbages. Once all of the cabbages are devoured, you win.

To mute/unmute Sound: Press "m"
To fire weapons (not yet implemented): Press "SPACEBAR"
