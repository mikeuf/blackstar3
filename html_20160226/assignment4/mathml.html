The Quadratic Formula

The ancient Babylonians developed the Quadratic Formula to help them predict what would happen if a negative boy is unable to decide if he wants to go to a radical party because he is a square, and he is going to miss four awesome chicks, and the party will be over at 2AM.

x = - b ± b 2 - 4 a c 2 a

Consider the following quadratic polynomial:

x 2 - 3 x - 4 = 0

One method of solving this is to "plug" the numbers into the quadratic formula, using this pattern:

a x 2 - b x - c = 0

This is what it looks like after the numbers have been "plugged" into the Quadratic Forumula:

x = - - 3 ± - 3 2 - 4 1 - 4 2 ( 1 )

This evaluates to the following:

x = 4 x = - 1
