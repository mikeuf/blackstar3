Instructions

This is the easiest (and dumbest) game ever. Just control your manatee by clicking the mouse, or tapping your screen. Try to eat all of the cabbages. Once all of the cabbages are devoured, you win.

To move the manatee: Click mouse or tap screen
To fire weapons (not yet implemented): Press "SPACEBAR"
