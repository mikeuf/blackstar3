About Me

I recently worked in Heathrow, Florida, as a "Knowledge Engineer" for a software company called Veritas Technologies. That's a fancy title for a hybrid role that connects technical writing, web design, a bit of software development, and occasional technical support. I enjoy projects that allow me to combine low-level, technical, skills with creative inspiration. Of course, that could mean just about anything these days, from a full stack developer to a rock star.

I completed a BA in Computer Science at Florida State University (FSU) in 2015 and am a current graduate student at University of Florida, working towards a master's degree in Mass Communication with a concentration in Web Design and Online Communication. This program combines Web Design, graphics design, and some traditional academic communication courses. It seems like a perfect way to build on the technical foundation that I gained while completing my undergraduate degree. I also hope to polish my writing skills and learn more about the emerging technologies and strategies that are being used to reach new audiences.

Between work and school, I haven't had much of a life, lately. I do enjoy listening to, and writing, music. If I'm feeling ambitious, I also enjoy cooking. I'm awful at both, but I still have fun. I like to jog. Unfortunately, I also like to eat. Maybe that's why I have the physique of Larry the Cable Guy, despite all the cardio.
