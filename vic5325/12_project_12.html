Project 12 - E-mail Campaign

14 November, 2017

 



This is a mock-up for an HBO email campaign. The purpose of the email is to get people to visit the HBO Now website and begin streaming popular HBO shows.

A challenge for this was getting everything to fit within the dimensions. I now have a greater appreciation for how many things typically are included in a professional email campaign. The font is mostly Gill Sans, except for the Game of Thrones font. The candy canes were originally red, but I used the color replacement brush to change some to green. The holiday references are intentionally a bit silly looking, especially when juxtaposed against the Night King -- the most serious guy in Westeros.

The television images are still from episodes of the series, except for Insecure, which is an actual title card. I drew the "play button" and reduced the opacity. For the other items, I mostly imitated the style of HBO promotional emails that I have received.

Disclaimer: This is just a school project. This website has no connection with HBO.
