Project 11 - Web Ads

7 November, 2017

 

Westworld is a popular television series by HBO. Recently, HBO has been promoting their "HBO Now" service that allows subscribers to watch content directly over the Internet, without cable.

A challenge with these advertisements is that there isn't much room to "tell a story." You have to make hard decisions about the key ideas are, and ensure that people will understand what you want them to do.

For this set of ads, there is a still shot from the show that gives a general feel for the fantasy element. The text is basically a teaser that hints that there is a darker side to this innocent-looking western scene. I tried to use a similar font (Copperplate) to the one used in the Westworld logo. The other key elements are clearly identifying the name of the series and leading the viewer's eyes toward the "call to action." In this case, the action is to click the banner to watch an excerpt from the series, via the new HBO Now service.
