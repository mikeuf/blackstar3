About

We build the sturdiest and prettiest ferris wheels east of the Mississippi. We also sell bouncy castles and steak knives. We are perhaps best known for selling the ferris wheel that was ridden by Jerry Falwell at the Chicago World's Fair. OK, he was actually riding a water slide, but he did once ride one of our ferris wheels. We promise!
